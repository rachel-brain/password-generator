Password Generator

A strong password contains:

  • between 8 & 128 characters; ideally, at least 15 characters
  • a mix of uppercase & lowercase letters
  • numbers
  • symbols such as ! ? $ % ( ) +

Additionally, a password should not be a dictionary word, a name (such as your kid's name or pet's name) nor should it be like any of your previous passwords.
